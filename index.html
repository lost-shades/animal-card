ANIMAL TRADING CARDS

SLOW LORIS

Species: Nycticebus

Habitat: Southeast Asia (forests and bamboo groves)

Diet: Omnivore - eats fruits, insects, small reptiles, and bird eggs

Average Lifespan: 10-20 years

Characteristics:The Slow Loris is a nocturnal primate known for its slow movements and large, round eyes. Despite its cute appearance, it possesses a venomous bite, making it one of the few venomous mammals. They are often illegally traded as pets, posing a threat to their populations in the wild.

Fun Fact: Slow Lorises have a toxic bite produced by glands located on their elbows, which they lick to deliver venom when threatened.

LION

Species: Panthera leo

Habitat: Grasslands, savannas, and open woodlands of Africa

Diet: Carnivore - preys on other animals

Average Lifespan: 10-14 years in the wild

Characteristics:The lion is a majestic big cat known for its golden mane (in males) and social behavior. It lives in prides led by a dominant male, with females doing most of the hunting. Lions are apex predators and play a crucial role in maintaining the balance of their ecosystem.

Fun Fact: Lions can roar as loud as 114 decibels, which can be heard up to 5 miles away!
